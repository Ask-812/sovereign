I've been thinking about this for a while:

The company — as we know it — is becoming optional.

AI collapsed the cost of building software. One person with AI agents can now produce what a 15-person team produced in 2020. The barriers to creating value have never been lower.

But here's what nobody talks about: the cost of OPERATING a business hasn't changed at all.

Legal entity formation: 2 weeks, $2,000, a lawyer.
Business bank account: requires incorporation.
Insurance: $5K/year, no category for "solo AI operator."
Contracts: $5K lawyer retainer.
Taxes: multi-jurisdiction nightmares.
Enterprise credibility: "You're just one person?"

The entire economic system — banks, insurance, procurement, regulation — is built around the company as the unit of production. But that unit is becoming unnecessary for more and more types of value creation.

So I'm building Sovereign: the operating system for independent operators.

Sign up → 60 seconds later, you're a full economic entity. Legal structure, bank account, payments, insurance, contracts, taxes — all handled by AI agents.

The most powerful feature: a Trust Profile that replaces "team size" as the credibility signal. Verified revenue, delivery track record, client satisfaction, compliance — based on real performance data, not self-reported claims. More transparent than a Fortune 500 vendor assessment.

And as the network grows — collective vendor pricing, cross-referrals, pooled insurance, shared brand trust. Independent, but never alone.

The future is independent. 1.57 billion freelancers on Earth. Tens of millions more trapped in jobs they'd leave if operating independently wasn't so hard.

AI gave them the ability to create. Sovereign gives them the ability to operate.
